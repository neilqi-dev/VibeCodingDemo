Report about high leaving rate of Parkside

Introduction
To investigate the reason why the leaving reate of retail store Parkside is higher than others, I interviewed the HR manager and staff working at Parkside and Highgate.

Findings
According to the statistics made by HR manager, the leaving number of Parkside and Highgate are basiclly equal, however the number of Parkside rushed to 20 while Highgate was down to only 2, which lead to severely insufficient of hands to cope with business, and the delivery date was added 2 days on average.

Suggestion
Parkside staff raised some ideas of improvement and some of them are reasonable, such as providing training classes and bonus schemes. With more training, it will not only level up staff's working ability, but also promote working productivity and minimum the cost of unregular operation. Further more, the company could reduce human resource cost and encourage staff working extra hours if we provide bonus schemes. 
However, the request about more paid hours is unacceptable, becuase Parkside is now under the hands-needed scenario.

conclustion
By offering training classes and more bonus plan, the staff retention of Parkside will be improved and the retail business there will be run smoothly.
